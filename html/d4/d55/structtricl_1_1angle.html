Detailed Description

An angle represents an indirect connection between a source entity and a target entity via two links through some "middle" entity.

When an angle is used, the source and target entities are clear from the context, hence they are not named themselves in the angle but only the middle entity and relationship or action type are stored.

Angles may influence the attempt rate or success probability of "adjacent" events (events with the same source and target entities).

The angle datatype may also be used to store an inleg by setting rat12 to NO_RAT or an outleg by setting rat23 to NO_RAT.
